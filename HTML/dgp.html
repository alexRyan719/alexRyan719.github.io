Don't Grape People

Mission Statement:
This is an idea for a song to deter sexual predation, as well as make amends for any past predation. The idea is for famous advocates to make their own verse and add to the trend. They can add their own twist or aspect to the song, or even speak about when it's happened to them. Then, others that have taken such shortcuts or trespasses can make their own verse (without naming names) to ask for forgiveness.
